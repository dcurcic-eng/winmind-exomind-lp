The Science Behind Your Recovery

What is Exomind?

Exomind is a second generation brain wellness therapy that uses patented ExoTMS™ technology to deliver precisely targeted magnetic pulses to the dorsolateral prefrontal cortex (DLPFC), the brain region responsible for mood regulation, decision making, and cognitive control.

FDA Cleared Technology

Exomind is FDA cleared for the treatment of Major Depressive Disorder, backed by rigorous clinical trials and proven safety data.

Non Invasive & Drug Free

No needles, no anesthesia, no medication side effects. Exomind works by gently stimulating your brain's natural neural pathways.

Under 30 Minutes

Each session takes less than 30 minutes. Walk in, sit comfortably, and walk out ready to continue your day with zero downtime.

Lasting Neuroplasticity

ExoTMS™ promotes lasting changes in brain connectivity. 58% of patients maintain sustained remission at 12 months post treatment.
